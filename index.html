About Us

Coffee&Code Belfast is a Meetup for those who are new to coding. We run monthly Saturday sessions, pairing / teaming up to work on coding challenges, and also practice development processes including Github collaboration. Each month, a guest mentor will help guide us through the challenge.

Our aim is to provide a fun, supportive and welcoming space to learn by doing, expand existing skills and meet others on a similar path. Anyone can attend, any age (18 to 100!), any level, especially those who are self-taught and would like to gain team experience away from online courses. Even if you're just thinking of a new career path as a developer, you can come along and see what it's all about!

Learning

Our events are an opportunity to practice existing skills and also add to your knowledge. The challenges are designed to be completed in each Meetup so you have another project to add to your portfolio!

Experience

Gain experience away from online courses and lectures, by joining in real world development tools and challenges. Experienced mentors are there to help guide you through!

Support

Meet others on a similar path and share knowledge! We encourage you to pair / team up to work through the coding challenge together. No matter what your level is, there is always something you can learn!
